Who is Alexander Bonilla?

Born in Maywood, Illinois, Alexander Bonilla's interest in computer science began at the University of Illinois at Chicago where he attained his Bachelor in Mathematics and Computer Science. The ease of building applications with any computer caught his attention and it has escalated from there. With his drive to learn more about the technological world, he began building projects for Sprouting Leaders (a non-profit organization) and for his friends. His curiosity to learn more has driven him to learn a variety of programming languages, which include: Python, C#, SQL, HTML, Typescript and many more. In order to continue his love for technology, he is always in search of new technologies and continues to find methods of including them into his projects.

Early Life

Growing up in Maywood, Alexander Bonilla attended high school at Proviso Mathematics and Science Academy. High school would be the place where he was exposed to the world of programming. In his junior year of high school, he attended a class which introduced him to the programming language, Java. Although he didn't quite understand his love for programming at the time, this class implanted the idea that this might be his branch of work for the future. Aside from education, Alexander spent his time playing multiple sports during high school, which include: soccer, taekwondo, wrestling and occasional basketball with friends. Athletics was a significant part of his life growing up. He took it as far as becoming the captain of the Proviso East soccer team and joining a traveling taekwondo team that took part in promoting the sport. It wasn't until he went to the University of Illinois at Chicago that he understood his love for programming.
