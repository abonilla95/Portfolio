Who is Alexander Bonilla?

Born in the surrounding suburbs of Chicago, Illinois, Alexander Bonilla's interest in computer science began at the University of Illinois at Chicago where he attained his Bachelor in Mathematics and Computer Science. The ease of building applications with any computer caught his attention and it has escalated from there. With his drive to learn more about the technological world, he began building projects for Sprouting Leaders (a non-profit organization), as an intern for Tecture and for his friends. His curiosity to learn more has driven him to learn a variety of programming languages, which include: Python, C#, SQL, HTML, Typescript and many more. In order to continue learning, he is always in search of new technologies and continues to find methods of including them into his projects.
